From their README:

ProximityHash generates a set of geohashes that cover a circular area, given the center coordinates and the radius.

With Geohashes, a list of lat/lons can be converted to a single dimension where shorting the hash gives nearby locations. This is fast and helpful for lookups/indexing spatial data.
