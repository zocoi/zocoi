Founded in February 2008, MahShelf has been built up as a universal platform for discovering and publishing online comics. MahShelf allows every kind of comic, manga and graphic book to be viewed comfortably in digital format. No need to wait each time, just continuous reading flow. At MahShelf, everyone can create their own library, explore and share reading taste with friends and family, or even publish their own digital comics.

The beta service was opened for public since June 2008. We are working hard to bring a fun, fresh, quick-reading environment for comic readers around the world!
