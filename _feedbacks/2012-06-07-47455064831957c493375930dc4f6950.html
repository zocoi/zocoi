Hey Hung,

I’m one of the co-founders of Timehop, a simple yet powerful product that answers the question "What did you do 1 year ago today?" We’re looking to add another awesome engineer to our tight-knit team of five so I thought I’d reach out.

Timehop helps people uncover their life story through their digital history by aggregating your personal data from Foursquare, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and text messages. So far, people really love us and we have big plans to do lots more.

Our team hails from Gilt Groupe (me!), Zynga, Groupme, Loosecubes and the Barbarian Group, with the same awesome investors as Twitter, Foursquare and Tumblr, and angels that include the Foursquare and Groupme founders.

This is a great opportunity for an engineer who wants to conceive, build, own and deploy projects from the ground up as part of a nimble team of five. We’re hoping our next addition to the team loves beer, karaoke, and creating new, exciting products as much as we do.

Are you free to jump on the phone Monday or Tuesday next week? Would love to learn more about what you’re doing and introduce you to Timehop more. Let me know if either day would work!

Best,
Benny
